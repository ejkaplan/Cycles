Instructions

When you hit run, your ship will appear on the blue square facing north. When you enter a square with a circle, the ship's program will be set to the corresponding color's program. Write the program by entering the characters 'F', 'R' and 'L' separated by commas. 'F' means go forward, 'R' means turn right and 'L' means turn left. The ship will carry out these commands in sequence, looping back to the first command if it runs out. When it lands on a circle and switches programs, it always starts from the start of the sequence, even if it was in the middle and even if it landed on the color of the program it was already running.

Try to land on the green square with the shortest (fewest instructions) program possible. If you try to move into a wall (dark grey) square, you will not move. If you move onto a lava (red) square, you fail the level.
